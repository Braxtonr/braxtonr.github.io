LeBron Raymone James is an American professional basketball player who is one of the greatest of all time and argueably the greatest of all time. His NBA resume includes three NBA championships, four NBA Most Valuable Player Awards, three NBA Finals MVP Awards, and two Olympic gold medals. He was drafted number one overall in 2003, right out of high school by his hometown team, the Cleveland Cavaliers. Aside from being an NBA superstar, LeBron is also a well known philanthropist. He invests the millions of dollars he makes from his profession into his community and uses his platform to speak on different social issues.
